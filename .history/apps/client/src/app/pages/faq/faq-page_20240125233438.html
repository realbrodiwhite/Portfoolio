Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find quick answers to commonly asked questions about Portfoolio in our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section. Discover what Portfoolio is, explore its features, and learn about our privacy practices. Get all the information you need in one place.

What is Portfoolio? Portfoolio is a lightweight, open source wealth management application for individuals to keep track of their net worth. The software empowers you to make solid, data-driven investment decisions. What assets can I track with Portfoolio? With Portfoolio, you can keep track of various assets like stocks, ETFs, bonds, cryptocurrencies and commodities. What else is included in Portfoolio? Please find a feature overview to manage your wealth here. How do I start? You can sign up via the “Get Started” button at the top of the page. You have multiple options to join Portfoolio: Create an account with a security token, using Internet Identity or Google Sign. We will guide you to set up your portfolio. Will you spam me with emails once I sign up? No, we do not even collect your email address, so you will not receive any spam emails from us. Can I use Portfoolio anonymously? Yes, the authentication systems (via security token or Internet Identity) enable you to sign in securely and anonymously to Portfoolio. There is no need for an e-mail address, phone number, or a username. How can Portfoolio be free? This project is driven by the efforts of contributors from around the world. The source code is fully available as open source software (OSS). Thanks to our generous Portfoolio Premium users and sponsors we have the ability to run a free, limited plan for novice investors. Is it really free? Yes, it is! Our pricing page details everything you get for free. Do you monetize or sell my financial data? No, we value your privacy. We do not sell or share your financial data with any third parties. What is your business model? By offering Portfoolio Premium, a subscription plan with a managed hosting service and enhanced features, we fund our business while providing added value to our users. What is Portfoolio Premium? Portfoolio Premium is a fully managed Portfoolio cloud offering for ambitious investors. Revenue is used to cover the costs of the hosting infrastructure and to fund ongoing development. It is the Open Source code base with some extras like the markets overview and a professional data provider. Can I start with a trial version? Yes, you can try Portfoolio Premium by signing up for Portfoolio and applying for a trial (see “My Portfoolio”). It is easy, free and there is no commitment. You can stop using it at any time. How can I get a student discount for Portfoolio Premium? Request your student discount here with your university e-mail address. Which devices are supported? Portfoolio works in every modern web browser on smartphones, tablets and desktop computers. For Android users, there is a dedicated Portfoolio app available in the Google Play Store. I cannot find my broker in the list of platforms. What can I do? Please send an e-mail with the web address of your broker to hi@ghostfol.io and we are happy to add it. Portfoolio sounds cool, how can I get involved? Any support for Portfoolio is welcome. Be it with a Portfoolio Premium subscription to finance the hosting infrastructure, a positive rating in the Google Play Store, a star on GitHub, feedback, bug reports, feature requests and of course contributions! You can reach us via Portfoolio Slack community, @portfoolio_, hi@ghostfol.io or GitHub. Got any other questions?
